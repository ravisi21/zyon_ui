Cancellation and Refund Policy

Understanding our cancellation and refund procedures.

Last updated: December 2024

1. Overview

At ZyonTrader, we strive to provide exceptional service and value to our customers. This policy outlines our procedures for cancellations and refunds for our trading platform services and subscriptions.

2. Subscription Cancellations

2.1 How to Cancel

You may cancel your subscription at any time through the following methods:

  • Log into your account and navigate to the subscription settings
  • Contact our customer support team via email
  • Submit a cancellation request through our online form

2.2 Cancellation Timeline

Once you submit a cancellation request:

  • Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period
  • You will continue to have access to all features until the cancellation takes effect
  • No new charges will be applied after the cancellation is processed
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the cancellation is complete

3. Refund Policy

3.1 Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be available under the following circumstances:

  • Service Issues: If our platform experiences significant downtime or technical issues
  • Billing Errors: In cases of duplicate charges or billing mistakes
  • Unauthorized Charges: For charges made without your consent

3.2 Non-Refundable Items

The following are generally not eligible for refunds:

  • Educational course purchases
  • Processing fees and bank charges

4. Refund Process

4.1 How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Contact our customer support team with your account details
  • Provide the reason for your refund request
  • Include any relevant documentation or screenshots
  • Allow 3-5 business days for review and processing

4.2 Refund Processing Time

Once approved, refunds are typically processed as follows:

  • Credit Card Refunds: 5-10 business days
  • Bank Transfer Refunds: 3-7 business days
  • Digital Wallet Refunds: 1-3 business days

5. Trading Account Cancellations

5.1 Account Closure

To close your trading account:

  • Ensure all open positions are closed
  • Withdraw any remaining funds
  • Submit a written request for account closure
  • Provide identification documents for verification

5.2 Account Closure Timeline

Account closure typically takes 5-10 business days to complete. During this time:

  • Your account will be restricted from new trading activity
  • You can still access your account to withdraw funds
  • All account data will be retained according to regulatory requirements

6. Partial Refunds

In certain circumstances, partial refunds may be available:

  • Unused Subscription Time: Pro-rated refunds for unused portions of annual subscriptions
  • Service Downtime: Credits or refunds for significant platform outages
  • Feature Removal: Refunds for features that are discontinued during your subscription

7. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision:

  • Contact our customer support team for review
  • Provide additional documentation if needed
  • Escalate to our management team if necessary
  • Consider mediation or arbitration for unresolved disputes

8. Special Circumstances

8.1 Force Majeure

In cases of force majeure (natural disasters, government actions, etc.), our standard refund policies may be modified to accommodate exceptional circumstances.

8.2 Regulatory Changes

Changes in financial regulations may affect our ability to provide certain services, potentially leading to refunds or service modifications.

9. Contact Information

For cancellation and refund requests, please contact us:

  • Email: refunds@zyontrader.com
  • Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567
  • Support Hours: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM EST
  • Online Form: Available 24/7 through your account dashboard

10. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this cancellation and refund policy at any time. Changes will be communicated to users through email notifications and platform updates. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of any policy changes.

11. Legal Rights

This policy does not affect your statutory rights under applicable consumer protection laws. You may have additional rights depending on your jurisdiction.
